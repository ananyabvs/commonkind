Wellbeing check-in

How have you been lately?

A short reflection — for you, not for anyone else. No scores, no diagnosis, no judgment.

Your privacy matters. This check-in is completely anonymous. No personal information is collected, no account is needed, and your responses are never linked to you in any way. Anonymous aggregate responses help us understand whether WarmHug is actually helping people — nothing more.
If you are in immediate distress, please reach out. Crisis Text Line: text HOME to 741741 (US/UK/Canada) · Befrienders Worldwide for international support.

Over the last two weeks — I have felt cheerful and in good spirits.

I have felt calm and relaxed.

I have felt active and vigorous.

I woke up feeling fresh and rested.

My daily life has been filled with things that interest me.

After spending time on this page, how do you feel compared to when you arrived?

Your response is anonymous and helps us understand if this space is working. No personal data is ever collected.

Our mission

Kindness is infrastructure.

WarmHug was built on the belief that in a time of global conflict and division, kindness is not soft — it is structural. A world without bullying is not a dream. It is buildable, one affirmation, one school, one community at a time.

This initiative was built with the goal of bringing kindness infrastructure into schools across the world — making encouragement permanently accessible to every student, everywhere. Every visitor to this page is part of that vision.
